### Exercise 2 — `rainbow()`

Give back **one row with six colored squares**, in exactly this order:

`red`, `orange`, `yellow`, `green`, `blue`, `purple`

```python
rainbow()   # -> [["red", "orange", "yellow", "green", "blue", "purple"]]
```

**Hint:** it's one row, so one pair of `[ ]` on the outside, and one more `[ ]` inside holding all six color words with commas between them. Don't forget the quotes around each color!

When it works, you'll see your rainbow painted in the Graph panel. That's your first picture made entirely out of code. 🌈
