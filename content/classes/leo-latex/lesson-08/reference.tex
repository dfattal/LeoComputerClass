\section{The Summit}

The last page of the book: five great constants meet, typeset by Leo.

%% eulers-identity
Set x equal to pi in the great split:

$$ e^{i\pi} = \cos(\pi) + i\sin(\pi) = -1 $$

%% impossible-logarithm
Take the logarithm of both sides — the impossible logarithm:

$$ \ln(-1) = i\pi $$

%% grand-finale
And close the way we opened — area, bracket, answer:

$$ \int_0^{\pi} \sin(x)\,dx = \left[ -\cos(x) \right]_0^{\pi} = 2 $$
