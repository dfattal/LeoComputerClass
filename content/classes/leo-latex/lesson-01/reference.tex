%% slope-machine
The derivative of $f(x) = x^2$, built straight from the limit:

$$ \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{(x+h)^2 - x^2}{h} = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{2xh + h^2}{h} = 2x $$

%% sqrt-integral
The exact area under the square-root curve:

$$ \int_0^1 \sqrt{x} \, dx = \left[ \frac{2}{3} x^{3/2} \right]_0^1 = \frac{2}{3} $$

%% log-integral
And one integral the power rule cannot touch:

$$ \int \frac{dx}{x+1} = \ln\left(x+1\right) + C $$
