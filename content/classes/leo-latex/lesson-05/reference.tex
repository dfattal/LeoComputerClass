\section{The Mirror Universe}

The natural logarithm — the exponential's undo button, typeset by Leo.

%% undo-button
Each one unwinds the other, both ways round:

$$ e^{\ln(x)} = \ln\left(e^x\right) = x $$

%% mirror-slope
The transcendental function whose slope is a plain fraction:

$$ \frac{d}{dx} \ln(x) = \frac{1}{x} $$

%% log-law
The mirror of the exponent rule — products become sums:

$$ \ln(ab) = \ln(a) + \ln(b) $$
