\section{The Rules of the Machine}

Derivatives of compound machines, typeset by Leo.

%% product-rule
A growing rectangle: width times height, each pulling its own weight.

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\left( x^2 \sin(x) \right) = 2x\sin(x) + x^2\cos(x) $$

%% chain-rule
Interlocked gears: the outer rate times the inner rate.

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\left( (x^2+1)^3 \right) = 3(x^2+1)^2 \cdot 2x = 6x(x^2+1)^2 $$

%% nested-root
A chain rule hiding under a roof.

$$ \frac{d}{dx} \sqrt{x^2+1} = \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+1}} $$
