\section{A New Dimension}

The number line grows a second axis, typeset by Leo.

%% the-rotation
One quarter-turn, done twice, points you backwards:

$$ i^2 = i \cdot i = -1 $$

%% conjugate-partners
A complex number times its mirror twin — watch the i's annihilate:

$$ (2+3i)(2-3i) = 4 - 9i^2 = 13 $$

%% quarter-turn
Multiplying by i IS a 90-degree rotation:

$$ i\left(3+2i\right) = 3i + 2i^2 = -2 + 3i $$
