\section{The Infinite Tower}

The exponential series, built brick by brick, typeset by Leo.

%% sum-machine
Sigma notation is just a spelled-out sum, folded up:

$$ 1 + x + \frac{x^2}{2!} + \frac{x^3}{3!} = \sum_{n=0}^{3} \frac{x^n}{n!} $$

%% infinite-tower
Let the tower grow forever and it becomes the most famous function there is:

$$ e^x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{n!} $$

%% self-replicating
And the reason it exists at all — it is its own derivative:

$$ \frac{d}{dx} e^x = e^x $$
