## Tips If You're Stuck

1. **Burn never stops (loops forever)?** Make sure you *subtract* mass each step: `m = m - burn_rate * dt`. If the mass doesn't shrink, the loop runs forever.
2. **Answer is `0`?** Check that you update `v` *inside* the loop, and `return v` at the end.
3. **`math.log` error?** It needs `import math` at the top (already there) — and `math.log` is the natural log, exactly what we want.
4. **Sim a bit off from the formula?** That's normal for Euler — a smaller `dt` gets you closer.
