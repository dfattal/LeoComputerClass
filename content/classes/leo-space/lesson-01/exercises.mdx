## Tips If You're Stuck

1. **Answer way too big or too small?** Make sure you squared `r` (use `r**2`, not just `r`). Forgetting the square is the #1 gravity bug.
2. **Exercise 2 not near 9.8?** Double-check you only put *one* mass on top (`M`), and that you're using Earth's radius `6.371e6` for `r`.
3. **`None` returned?** Don't forget `return` at the start of each function's answer.
4. **Using the right G?** It should be `6.674e-11` — already defined for you at the top. Just write `G` in your formula.
