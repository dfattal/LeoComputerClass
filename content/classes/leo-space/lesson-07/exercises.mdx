## Tips If You're Stuck

1. **`burn_one` way off?** Double-check the vis-viva part: `2/r1 - 1/a`, all multiplied by `G*M`, then square-rooted.
2. **Use the Sun's mass** (`1.989e30`), not Earth's — you're orbiting the *Sun* now.
3. **`transfer_time` huge?** That's expected — it's in *seconds*. Divide by 86400 to get days.
4. **`a**3` inside the sqrt** — keep `a**3 / (G * M)` together under `math.sqrt`.
