## Tips If You're Stuck

1. **Apogee comes out as just `r0`?** You need *more steps* — a stretched orbit takes longer to reach the top. Or your launch speed isn't above circular.
2. **Same 2D loop as Lesson 2** — gravity points inward with `r**3` on the bottom, velocity before position.
3. **Period way off?** Make sure `a**3 / (G*M)` is *all* inside `math.sqrt`, and you multiplied by `2*math.pi`.
4. **`None` returned?** Don't forget the `return` lines.
