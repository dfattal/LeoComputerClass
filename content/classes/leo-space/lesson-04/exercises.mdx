## Tips If You're Stuck

1. **`will_escape` always True (or always False)?** Check the sign in the energy: it's `+0.5*v**2` *minus* `G*M/r`.
2. **Escape speed too small?** Don't forget the `2` on top — escape speed is bigger than orbital speed.
3. **Compare your answers:** `escape_speed` should be about 1.41× the `orbital_speed` from Lesson 2 at the same radius.
4. **`None` returned?** Don't forget the `return` lines.
